Projects

Infectious Disease (Malaria) Modelling (Mar 2019 - July 2020)

This project involved developing a population model that integrates within-host dynamics to explore the complex epidemic behaviors of Malaria parasites. The model, which is based on a customized agent-based approach and implemented in Python, provides insights into the intricate lifecycle of Malaria and its impact on population-level disease transmission.

Bluetooth Map Matching (April 2021 - Present)

This project tackles the challenges associated with Bluetooth map-matching, focusing on issues like the high complexity of large road networks, the absence of ground-truth data, and the varying levels and types of noise. The work has resulted in the publication of a paper, which can be accessed here: IEEE Xplore.
